Mimi's Tikka Masala

Description

My personal favourite, loved by many, invented in the United Kingdom, inspired by Indian cuisine, just a must try!

The heavily spiced cream tomato sauce, with pieces of chicken thighs, onions and coconut flavor make this dish a must try for every homecook.

It might look difficult to make, but trust me, with the following instructions this recipe becomes a breeze. Don't wait and treat yourself with this amazing dish!

Ingredients

  • 600g boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 cans of crushed/minced/chopped tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • medium sized knob of (grated) ginger
  • 2dl heavy cream
  • 250g butter
  • 2 tbsp Meira Garam Masala spice mix (the right amount, not the white amount)
  • 1 tbsp paprika spice
  • 1-2 tsp chili flakes
  • 100ml of Monin Coconut syrup
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional secret tip: 1Â½ tsp of MSG (the king of flavor)

Instructions

  1. Dice onion, grate ginger and cut chicken into smaller pieces
  2. Add oil to wok or pan and sear on medium-high heat for ~5mins
  3. When ready take them out to rest on the side
  4. Lower heat to medium, then add some butter, the diced onion and ginger for ~3mins (Make sure the butter and onions do not burn)
  5. When onions are soft add the paprika spice and garam masala
  6. Add both cans of tomato
  7. Add chicken back
  8. Let simmer on medium-low heat for ~15mins
  9. Add rest of butter, cream and coconut syrup
  10. Flavor explosion!
